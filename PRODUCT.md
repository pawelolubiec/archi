# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Milarex board members and executives reviewing the company’s digital direction and the decisions required to carry it forward.

## Product Purpose

An interactive board presentation that makes Milarex’s digital strategy understandable, credible, and durable. It should secure approval now while remaining useful as the shared roadmap for 2026–2030.

## Positioning

One connected view of the business, architecture, initiatives, and execution path required to deliver Milarex’s 2026–2030 digital strategy.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: approve the digital strategy and its key decisions.
- Secondary CTA: use the presentation as the reference roadmap for subsequent planning and governance.
- The line a viewer remembers after 10 seconds: Milarex has a coherent, executable digital roadmap through 2030.
- Belief ladder: the current constraints are material; the target architecture addresses them; the initiative portfolio is prioritized; the roadmap is executable; the requested decisions unlock delivery.
- Proof on hand: the connected architecture model, initiative portfolio, KPI targets, and 2026–2030 roadmap contained in the presentation.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, and cinematic. The tone is concise and board-ready: strategically ambitious without becoming theatrical or speculative.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a generic AI dashboard or a template-generated presentation deck.

## Design Principles

- Make the decision and its business consequence immediately legible.
- Connect strategy, architecture, investment, and execution into one narrative.
- Use interaction to reveal detail without weakening the board-level message.
- Preserve enough structure and clarity for the presentation to remain useful through 2030.
- Earn confidence through specificity rather than visual noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal accessibility target has been specified.
